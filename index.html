Movie Single-Genre Classifier

Type in any movie plot summary and we will predict the movie's genre. The single-genre classifier only supports Action, Romance, and Comedy genres.


Movie Multi-Genre Classifier

Type in any movie plot summary and we will predict the movie's genres. This classifier can predict many genres for your plot.


Movie Reviews Sentiment Analysis

Construct a review for a movie and we will predict whether it is a positive or negative review.


Movie Preference Predictor

This is a movie preference predictor based on relevant feedback loop. We will sample a random movie and predict whether you like it or not. Then, you can tell us your actual preference. After more feedback from you, our predictor can be trained to make more accurate prediction about your preference.


The predictor is trained using relevance feedback techniques using either nearest centroid or k-nearest neighbors based on tf_idf term vectors extracted from movie plot summary.
